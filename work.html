what i do

i work as a freelance photographer, social media strategist, and website designer. i am presently employed at the cartersville farmers market, where i work as the social & digital media strategist. my responsibilities include: designing and maintaining our website, directing our social media campaigns, photographing our charming market, and assisting our vendors with cultivating an online presence of their own.

i am also employed as the marketing intern of north cobb christian school, where i presently attend high school. i work alongside a small marketing and production team to help create content for our social media channels, develop and maintain comprehensible brand guidelines for internal use, coordinate and produce school events—such as graduation, and write copy for press releases and other external memos.

in the past, i have worked as a contract, special-events photographer for the downtown cartersville development authority.
